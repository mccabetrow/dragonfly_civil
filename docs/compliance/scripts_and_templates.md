## 1. Phone Scripts

### 1.1 Initial Contact Call

**Objective:** Make first contact, deliver Mini-Miranda, assess payment ability

```
OPENING:
"Hello, this is [YOUR NAME] calling from Dragonfly Civil.
May I speak with [DEBTOR NAME]?"

[If confirmed speaking with debtor]

"Thank you. I'm calling regarding a court judgment entered against you
in [COURT NAME], case number [CASE NUMBER].

This is an attempt to collect a debt and any information obtained
will be used for that purpose.

The current balance owed is $[AMOUNT], which includes the original
judgment of $[PRINCIPAL] plus accrued interest.

I'm calling to discuss resolution options. Are you able to pay this
balance in full, or would you like to discuss a payment arrangement?"
```

**Response Branches:**

**If "I can pay in full":**

```
"That's great. I can take payment information now or provide
instructions for sending a check or money order. Which would you prefer?"

[Collect payment information per approved methods]

"Thank you. You'll receive written confirmation within [X] days."
```

**If "I need a payment plan":**

```
"I understand. Let me ask a few questions to see what we can work out.

What would be a comfortable monthly payment for you?
[Listen and note]

Based on that, I can offer [CALCULATED PLAN] over [X] months.
Does that work for you?

[If yes] Great, let me confirm the details and we'll send written confirmation.
[If no] What would work better for your situation?"
```

**If "I dispute this" or "This isn't mine":**

```
"I understand. I'm going to note that you're disputing this debt.
We'll pause collection and send you written verification of the judgment.

What's the best mailing address for that?
[Confirm address]

You should receive that within 5-7 business days.
Do you have any questions about that process?"
```

**If "I can't pay anything":**

```
"I understand times can be difficult. Can you tell me a little about
your situation?
[Listen without judgment]

Even a small amount regularly can help resolve this over time.
Is there any amount you could manage monthly?

[If hardship is severe] I'm going to note your situation.
We may need to explore other options. Someone from our office
may follow up with you."
```

### 1.2 Follow-Up Call (Promised Payment Not Received)

**Objective:** Inquire about missed commitment without being aggressive

```
OPENING:
"Hello, this is [YOUR NAME] from Dragonfly Civil.
May I speak with [DEBTOR NAME]?"

[If confirmed]

"I'm following up on our conversation from [DATE] regarding the
judgment in case [CASE NUMBER].

At that time, you indicated you would [send a payment / set up a plan]
by [DATE]. I don't see that we've received that yet.

Is everything okay? Were you able to arrange that payment?"
```

**Response Branches:**

**If "I forgot / got busy":**

```
"I understand things come up. Can we get that taken care of today?
I can take payment information right now, or we can set a new date
that works better for you."
```

**If "I couldn't afford it":**

```
"I'm sorry to hear that. Has your situation changed since we last spoke?
Let's see if we can adjust the arrangement to something more manageable."
```

**If "I sent it":**

```
"Thank you for letting me know. Sometimes there's a delay in processing.
Can you tell me when and how you sent it?
[Note details]
I'll check on our end and follow up if there's an issue."
```

### 1.4 Voicemail Script (Privacy-Compliant)

**Objective:** Request callback without disclosing debt information

```
"Hello, this is [YOUR NAME] from Dragonfly Civil calling for [DEBTOR NAME].

Please return my call at [PHONE NUMBER] regarding an important matter.

Again, that number is [PHONE NUMBER].

Thank you."
```

**⚠️ DO NOT mention:**

- Debt or money owed
- Court judgment
- Legal action
- Any financial details

## 2. Demand Letters

### 2.1 Initial Demand Letter (With Validation Notice)

```
[DRAGONFLY CIVIL LLC LETTERHEAD]

[DATE]

[DEBTOR NAME]
[DEBTOR ADDRESS]

RE: Court Judgment – Case No. [CASE NUMBER]
    Original Creditor: [CREDITOR NAME]
    Current Balance Due: $[TOTAL AMOUNT]

Dear [DEBTOR NAME]:

Our firm has acquired the judgment entered against you in [COURT NAME]
on [JUDGMENT DATE]. We are writing to notify you of this judgment and
to request payment.

AMOUNT OF DEBT:
  Original Judgment:        $[PRINCIPAL]
  Interest to Date:         $[INTEREST]
  Court Costs:              $[COSTS]
  TOTAL AMOUNT DUE:         $[TOTAL]

This debt is owed to [ORIGINAL CREDITOR NAME / DRAGONFLY CIVIL LLC].

IMPORTANT NOTICE ABOUT YOUR RIGHTS:

Unless you notify us within 30 days after receiving this letter that
you dispute the validity of this debt or any portion thereof, we will
assume this debt is valid.

If you notify us in writing within 30 days that you dispute the debt,
we will obtain verification of the debt and mail you a copy.

If you request in writing within 30 days, we will provide you with the
name and address of the original creditor, if different from the current
creditor.

PAYMENT OPTIONS:

We encourage you to resolve this matter to avoid further enforcement
action. You may:

1. Pay in full by [DATE - 30 days out]
2. Contact us to discuss a payment arrangement
3. Dispute this debt in writing within 30 days

Payment may be made by check or money order payable to Dragonfly Civil LLC
and mailed to the address above, or by calling us to arrange electronic payment.

THIS IS AN ATTEMPT TO COLLECT A DEBT. ANY INFORMATION OBTAINED WILL BE
USED FOR THAT PURPOSE. THIS COMMUNICATION IS FROM A DEBT COLLECTOR.

Sincerely,

[SIGNATURE]
[NAME]
Dragonfly Civil LLC
[PHONE NUMBER]
```

This offer is contingent upon receipt of $[SETTLEMENT AMOUNT] on or

Payment must be made by:
• Certified check or money order
• Electronic transfer (contact us for instructions)

• We will mark your account as "Settled in Full"
• We will file a Satisfaction of Judgment with the court
• No further collection activity will occur on this account

• Partial payments do not constitute acceptance
• This offer may not be extended or renewed
• If payment is not received by the deadline, the full balance remains due

To accept this offer, send payment to the address above with a copy of
this letter, or contact us to arrange electronic payment.

## Appendix: Compliance Notes

### Template Usage Rules

1. **Required Elements:** Do not remove Mini-Miranda, validation rights, or debt collector disclosure from any communication
2. **Customization:** Adapt specifics (names, amounts, dates) but keep legal language intact
3. **Review:** Any significant modification requires compliance review
4. **Timing:** Validation notice must be sent within 5 days of initial contact if not included
5. **Documentation:** Log all communications sent with date, method, and content reference

### Prohibited Modifications

Never add:

- Threats of criminal action
- False urgency language ("immediate arrest," "final warning" if not true)
- Statements about credit reporting (unless accurate and appropriate)
- Language suggesting legal action unless actually planned
- Personal opinions or emotional appeals

### Record Retention

Keep copies of all communications for:

- Active accounts: Until 1 year after resolution
- Settled/paid accounts: 7 years from resolution
- Disputed accounts: 7 years from final resolution
